How does it work?

1. Your eco-conscious neighbor decides to rent out their EV charger during off-use hours. Our AI-powered pricing engine suggests a suitable rate, taking into account real-time electricity costs and the charger's location popularity.


2. An EV owner in need of a charge turns to our app, locating nearby chargers with ease. A few clicks and credit card details later, the charging begins. Users can set limits on the cost of a charging session or the amount of kWh to be charged.


3. Users can actively monitor the charging process directly within the app, with the freedom to halt the charging session at their convenience.


4. Once the charging session concludes, the total cost is calculated and disbursed to the charger renter within a few days, making it a hassle-free and efficient process.
